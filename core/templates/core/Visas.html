Frequently Asked Questions

A visa is an official document that allows you to enter, stay, and exit a country for a specific purpose and duration. It is issued by the government of the country you wish to visit.
To apply for a visa, you need to fill out an application form, provide the required documents, and pay the visa fee. The application can be submitted online or at the nearest embassy or consulate of the country you wish to visit.
The documents required for a visa application vary depending on the country and the type of visa you are applying for. Generally, you will need to provide a valid passport, passport-sized photos, proof of accommodation, and a travel itinerary.
The processing time for a visa varies depending on the country and the type of visa. It can take anywhere from a few days to several weeks. It is advisable to apply for a visa well in advance of your planned travel date.
